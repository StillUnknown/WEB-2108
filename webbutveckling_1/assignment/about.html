About me

I was born on the 29th of June 1985 in a town in Sweden called Trollhättan. With a quick use of math we can easily see that it makes me 36 years old today. I grew up in a really tiny town called Magra. I can't recall its exact location on the top of my head, but it's in the vicinity of Sollebrunn. My sister was born a year later, and we spent our next six years in Magra. Eventually my mother and father separated due to my father meeting someone else. There was a big drama which I won't go into here, but it ended up with my mother, me and my sister moving to a suburb to Gothenburg called Ytterby.

I spent the next 16 years in Ytterby but when I got an offer to rent an apartment in Gothenburg via a friend I couldn't say no. That a girl I was interested in lived only a few minutes from that place didn't exactly made the decision harder ;)
This girl and I work at the same company, so I'd been checking her out for a couple for years without really trying to make a move on her. I've only sent her a couple of text maybe. Well, happy coincidence, almost the same time as I moved to the apartment our company had a big party which we both attended. Later that night we both ended up at the bar, and it quickly got awkward because I'm really shy when it comes to speaking with girls and it ended up with me not saying a word to her..
Well, the night passed, and I felt like an idiot. Luckily this girl texted me after a week and called me out. Said I was a chicken for not speaking to her, which is true! She later same night called me and asked if I could drive her home from a party she attended and ofcourse i could :D

If you haven't guessed it by now this girl and I ended up together. I moved in with her three months later and she got pregnant a month after that. Ten months later our first daughter was born. We soon felt that her apartment was a bit too small for us since we knew we wanted more children so we started looking for houses. We saw a plot was for sale in Ytterby and on a whim we decided to buy it and build a house. We later got three additional kids so now our family consists of:

  • Liselott, 40yrs
  • Christoffer, 36yrs
  • Linnéa, 11yrs
  • Alicia, 10yrs
  • Noella, 6yrs
  • Casper, 2yrs

What my experience with tech looks like

Since the day i could figure out what tech was, it grabbed me and never let go. I still remember our first TV my mother bought to our apartment we moved into after my parents separated. Let me tell you one thing, it doesn't work as modern TV's do. Back then, you had to manually find the frequency of every channel and then set it to the correct channelnumber.
